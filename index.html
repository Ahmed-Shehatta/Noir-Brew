Our Story

Crafted with obsession,
served with warmth

Born from a late-night dream and a suitcase full of Ethiopian beans, Noir & Brew opened its doors in 2019 with one simple belief: great coffee changes the way you see the day.

Every cup we serve is the result of weeks of sourcing, roasting, and refining — a relationship between farmer, roaster, and barista that spans continents.
